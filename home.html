GAMATHON is one of the most popular gaming conventions around the world and is a much anticipated eSports event of 2020. This tournament is hosted by Indian video game developers,RBS Entertainment. This is also one of the older annual eSports events and has been hosted every year since 2013. It sees a huge number of participants and for the 2020 tournament, we can expect around 40,000 or more gamers to enter this tournament. Unlike the other eSports events ,GAMATHON actually features several games.Participants can compete in VALORANT,CS:GO,PUBG and the FORTNITE tournaments.
